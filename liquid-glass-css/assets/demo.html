Liquid Glass

Apple's recipe = less blur + more refraction + a bright Fresnel bezel, over rich content. The hero card stacks .glass--clear (thins the frost so light bends instead of scattering) + .glass--apple — the pure-CSS/SVG ceiling: a no-JS gradient-map barrel refraction, real chromatic aberration, and a feSpecularLighting glint — + .glass--fresnel for the rim.
